Our Core Principle

FolkLore AI is built on a simple principle: your thoughts belong to you. We've designed our Chrome extension to work entirely within your browser, ensuring your data never touches our servers unless you explicitly choose to use optional features.

Zero Knowledge Architecture

We literally cannot see your data. Everything is processed locally in your browser or stored in your personal cloud accounts. We have no access to your highlights, notes, or browsing activity.

2

What Happens Locally

When you highlight text, everything happens locally in your browser. FolkLore AI processes:

üìù Your Browser Processes

  • Selected text - The content you highlight
  • Page URL & title - To remember where you found it
  • Context - Surrounding text for better understanding
  • Timestamp - When you saved it
  • Your notes - Any annotations you add

üìç Stays On Your Device

  • Chrome Local Storage - Never leaves your device
  • Your Dropbox - If you enable sync (optional)
  • Zero server storage - We literally can't see your data
3

Third-Party Services

FolkLore AI integrates with optional third-party services to enhance functionality:

OpenAI (Optional)

  • Only activated when you click "Activate AI" and provide your API key
  • Your browser communicates directly with OpenAI
  • We never see or store your API key
  • AI processing happens only on demand

Dropbox (Optional)

  • Used for cross-device synchronization
  • You authenticate directly with Dropbox
  • Data stored in your personal Dropbox account
  • We never access your Dropbox files
Important Note

Both services are completely optional. FolkLore AI works fully offline without them. When you do use them, your data goes directly from your browser to these services - it never passes through our servers.

4

Data Security

Your data security is ensured through:

  • Browser Security - Chrome's secure storage APIs protect your local data
  • Encryption in Transit - All communications use HTTPS
  • No Central Database - We can't be hacked for data we don't have
  • OAuth 2.0 - Secure authentication for optional services
5

Your Rights & Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • View Your Data - See everything stored locally anytime
  • Export Everything - Download all your sessions and annotations
  • Delete Selectively - Remove individual highlights or entire sessions
  • Disconnect Services - Revoke access to Dropbox or clear API keys
  • Complete Removal - Uninstall the extension to delete all local data
