Plain English Summary

FolkLore AI is a Chrome extension that helps you save and organize web content. Here's what you need to know:

1. What We Do

FolkLore AI is a Chrome extension that:

  • Lets you highlight and save text from websites
  • Uses AI to find connections between your saved content
  • Provides AI chat features to help you brainstorm

2. Your Content

Simple: You own everything you save. We don't claim any ownership of your annotations, notes, or ideas. We just store them so you can access them later.

3. Privacy

We take privacy seriously:

  • Your data is encrypted
  • We don't sell your data
  • We don't share it with third parties
  • You can delete everything anytime

See our Privacy Policy for details.
